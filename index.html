Who Am I?

Hi there! My name is Joni and I am a senior web developer at Georgetown University in Washington DC. I specialize in developing CMS themes and Javascript applications, and have spent the last 20 years building solutions for higher education, e-commerce corporations, non-profit organizations, and technology companies.

I am also a single mother by choice and the author of Sweet Little You, a welcome baby book for single parents and their donor-conceived children. In my rapidly dwindling spare time, I enjoy live music, knitting, and baking.
