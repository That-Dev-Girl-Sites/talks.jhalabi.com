About Joni

I am a “middle-end” web developer at Georgetown University who likes to write code, commit often, and make up her own titles. I have spent the last 20 years developing solutions for a wide variety of industries and organizations. I currently specialize in developing CMS themes and WordPress editor customizations.

When I am not writing code, I write technical articles and creative works focusing on life as a single mother by choice. I am the author of Sweet Little You, a welcome baby book for single parents and their donor-conceived children.

In my rapidly dwindling spare time, I enjoy running, practicing yoga, reading, and seeing live music near my home in the Washington, D.C. area.
